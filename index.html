FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does it take to build a website?
+
Starter websites: 3–5 days. Business websites: 5–7 days. Enterprise/e-commerce: 10–14 days. Timelines start after we receive all your content and 50% advance payment.
What's included in the price?
+
All our packages include domain, hosting, SSL certificate, mobile-responsive design, and 1 year of support. No hidden fees — what you see is what you pay.
How many revisions do I get?
+
We offer unlimited revisions during the design phase. After development begins, we include 3 rounds of revisions. We want you to love the final product — we don't stop until you do.
Do you provide hosting?
+
Yes! All packages include 1 year of fast, reliable hosting on Hostinger with 99.9% uptime guarantee. After the first year, renewal is available at competitive rates.
Will my website rank on Google?
+
Every website we build follows SEO best practices — fast loading, semantic HTML, meta tags, and mobile optimization. Business and Enterprise packages include additional SEO setup to accelerate rankings.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept UPI, bank transfer, and all major payment apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm). Payment is 50% upfront to start, and 50% upon delivery.
